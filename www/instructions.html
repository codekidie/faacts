There are two types of test in the assessment. First, is the personality intersest test and second is the aptitude test which has 9 sub-tests.

Answering the assessment test:

1. Tap the radio button of your answer. The shaded color button indicates your choosen answer.

2. Scroll down to navigate to another questions.

3.After answering all the questions tap the submit button to update your result.

4.Tap the next button to navigate to another assessment test.The result will be shown after you answer the personality and interest test and the aptitude test.
