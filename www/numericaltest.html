1. It takes about 30 days for Mel to finish her project. This is about 2/3 the time it takes for Rhoel and Joy, working together, to finish their projects. If it takes Joy half the time as it takes Mel, in how many days will Rhoel finish his project?

2. In the MSA department store, a crate of apples consists of t apples, and p apples cost q pesos. If I have r pesos, how much change will I receive from buying s crates of apples?
